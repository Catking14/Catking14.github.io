Structual Analysis

Thesis Statement

In the last paragraph [Sec. #6], the last sentence as highlighted in the following quotes would be the thesis statement for these reasons. First, it contains the main idea throughout the entire essay Here, people act as nothing is happening, which emphasize the topic of the essay: 'Nothing has really changed': In Moscow, fighting is a world away again. Besides, the comparison between "here" and "there" enchanced the impression of the article.

Take-home Message

In the second paragraph of the third section, the author shows that although the daily life of Muscovites seems still the same, but the war is still there. Under the surface of normal life, there are still countless of people are struggling in different ways. Although that we usually thought that Russians start the war, not everyone are willing to join the fight. Sometimes, people can't see the whole picture of things in this scale, not because they don't want, but they can't. Just like the author said, The fact that you exist and have your opinion doesn't mean anything. There are five, 10, 20 million of us. And it doesn't make any difference. These people are not blind to know what is happening, but striving to live on. After so many different perspectives the author has shown to us, we can see that the author is trying to tell us that there are no absolute wrong or right, but the war stays there. If there is a war, there are people suffering, no matter which side they are.
